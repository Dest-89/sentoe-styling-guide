Grooming
Checklist

Small details make a big difference in how polished you look on camera.

07

Hair

Get a haircut 5-7 days before. Style the morning of your session as you normally would.

Skin

Stay hydrated. No new skincare products the week before. Get plenty of rest.

Makeup

Natural, matte finish works best. Avoid shimmer or glitter. We can do minor touch-ups.

Nails

Clean, trimmed nails. Neutral polish if any. Hands often appear in photos.

Facial Hair

Men: Shave or trim morning of. Clean lines photograph better than stubble.

Glasses

Clean lenses thoroughly. Consider anti-glare or contacts if glare is an issue.

Session Day Tips

1

Trust the Process

I'll guide you through every pose. You don't need to know what to do with your hands.

2

Move Slowly

Small movements look best on camera. Subtle shifts create natural, relaxed images.

3

Breathe & Relax

Take deep breaths between shots. Relax your shoulders. Unclench your jaw.

4

Think Happy Thoughts

Real smiles come from real feelings. Think of someone who makes you laugh.

5

Ask Questions
